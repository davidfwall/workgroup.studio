Thank you and goodbye

25.10.2019

On March 1st 2006, we started a design studio; hopeful and excited for what lay in store. Today, we close the studio with the same mindset, confident that the most exciting challenges are the ones ahead.

While we feel some inevitable sadness, our overriding sentiment is gratitude. We’re thankful for the immense privilege to have done what we love alongside wonderful people, and to create work of which we’re so proud.

For the next leg of our journeys, our starting point is defined by the many friendships and achievements of the past 13 years. What a great place to begin.
